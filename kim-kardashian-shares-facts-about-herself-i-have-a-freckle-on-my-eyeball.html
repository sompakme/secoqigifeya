Kim Kardashian Shares Facts About Herself: I Have a Freckle on My Eyeball

Back at it again. Kim Kardashian took to her app on Friday, February 3, to share 20 unknown facts about herself, including the revelation that she has a freckle on her eyeball. (Kardashian was also, incidentally, Us Weekly‘s first featured celebrity in its “25 Things” franchise, in the February 23, 2009, issue!)

In addition to the odd birthmark, Kardashian, 36, also revealed that she has six piercings — “one belly button, one in right ear, 3 on my left lobe and one really high on left ear” — and a tendency to sleep with her eyes “slightly open,” which “scares the s–t out of people.”

The reality star’s list also includes personal grooming habits (“I’m obsessed with my nails and cuticles. If my nails are chipped or not perfect, I feel filthy”) and a confirmation that she and husband Kanye West are soulmates (“I love only hot rooms and hate when the air conditioning is on. I knew Kanye and I were meant to be when I slept over and he had the heat on”).

Back in 2009, Kardashian’s confessions included a sweet tooth (“I love cookies-and-cream ice cream,” she told Us) and an obsession with her teeth (“I’m obsessed with teeth and really want to bleach mine”). She also said she wanted to have a big family of her own one day (“I want a lot of kids, like four or five!” she said at the time).

Kardashian’s candid confessions come after her reemergence on social media and going out and about with family. On Sunday, January 29, the Selfish author was spotted flaunting her toned figure in a black two-piece bikini while on vacation with the Kardashian-Jenner clan in Costa Rica.

Later that evening, she stepped out for dinner with older sister Kourtney, flashing her nipples in a sheer pink bodysuit and semisheer striped black pants.

The mother of two kept a relatively low profile in the months following her terrifying Paris robbery in October and West’s hospitalization in November, steering clear of social media and lying low over the holidays.
