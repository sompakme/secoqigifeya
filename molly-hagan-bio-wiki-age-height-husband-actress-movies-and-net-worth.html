Molly Hagan Biography

Molly Hagan is an American actress who was born in Minneapolis, Minnesota, United States. She always wanted to be an actress. She toiled with her sister, Lucy Hagan, to create the best living room theatre a family could watch. Molly is widely known for her roles in television on Herman’s Head(1991–1994) and Unfabulous. She also co-starred in films including Some Kind of Wonderful(1987), Code of Silence(1985), Sully(2016), and Election(1999)

Molly Actress

Molly portrayed Diana Luna alongside Chuck Norris on the 1985 action film Code of Silence. She has appeared in several television series and film including Some Kind of Wonderful and Dallas: The Early Years. She starred in the cult series Nutt House, which had a six-week run in 1989, and Herman’s Head, which ran on Fox from 1991 to 1994. She also played Angel, one of the four characters inside Herman, and represented his sensitivity.

Molly appeared in 2014  as the lead character’s mother in The CW series, iZombie, based on the DC Comics. She later made recurring guest appearances in Jane the Virgin, The Orville, Truth Be Told and No Good Nick. Molly co-starred in 2017 in the NBC limited series Law & Order True Crime. She appeared in a series-regular role as title character mother in the CW crime series, Walker in 2020
