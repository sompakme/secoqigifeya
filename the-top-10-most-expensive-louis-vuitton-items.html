The Top 10 Most Expensive Louis Vuitton Items

Considered as one of the most prestigious brand names of all time, Louis Vuitton represents the very essence of luxury. While its price may seem out of this world, the brand carries not only prestige but also the assurance of quality.

Louis Vuitton items can only be found in specialty boutiques and upscale shops, thus further emphasizing the exclusivity of its products. Items from decades back are still highly valued so long as it carries the prestigious luxury brand’s name. The craftsmanship of its products is impeccable, making the Louis Vuitton brand name one of the most envied in the industry.

So long as the LV emblem is attached to it, a pair of women’s shoes that cost thousands of dollars suddenly become worth it. The company also once created a pair of shoes for men that had a price tag of $10,000. It has been praised ever since it was introduced in 2010 as part of the brand’s spring and summer collection.

Even golf bags become highly sought after items if the company created them. Louis Vuitton golf bags cost more than $9,000. Buyers are given four golf tees along with the bag.

With its wide variety of products, you may wonder what are the priciest creations of the company. Here now is a list of the top 10 most expensive Louis Vuitton items.

10. Louis Vuitton Tambour Bijou Secret Pink Gold Womens Watch - $26,700

The Louis Vuitton Tambour Bijou Secret Pink Gold watch is made for women. It features a monogram flower that will slide off to reveal the watch. The pink gold watch features 183 diamonds and grey mother of pearl. The watch also has a bespoke steel time adjustment tool.

9. Louis Vuitton Tambour Bijou Secret White Gold Women’s Watch - $26,700

The Louis Vuitton Tambour Bijou Secret White Gold is a woman’s watch that has a monogram flower which hides the face. The cover is made out of a combination of mother of pearl, gold and 183 diamonds. There are two interchangeable straps available. The first one is made of white alligator leather while the second is of triple coiled white Monogram Vernis leather. It has ardillon buckle. The watch measures 18 mm in diameter and has quartz movement and bespoke steel time adjustment tool.

8. Louis Vuitton Tambour Voyagez Automatic Chronograph Pink Gold Mens Watch - $31,000

The Louis Vuitton Tambour Voyagez Automatic Chronograph Pink Gold watch is a mans watch that took its inspiration from the dashboards of classic vehicles. It has horizontal chronograph counters and tachymeter functions. The watch has a grey dial and measures 44 mm in diameter. It is waterproof up to a distance of 100 meters. The face is covered by a sapphire glass with reflection-proof coating. It also features luminescent hands and a big date at 12 o’clock.

7. Louis Vuitton Patchwork Bag - $45,000

The Louis Vuitton Patchwork bag was made out of materials from 15 other Louis Vuitton bags. Only 24 pieces were ever made. Despite the weird design and patchy look, the bag still sold out after it was released.

6. Louis Vuitton Campanas Maracatu Furniture - $51,500

The Louis Vuitton Campanas Maracatu furniture is a folding travel cabinet. Users can easily hang the item on the wall. The furniture was conceptualized and created by Fernando and Humberto Campana, a design duo from Brazil. It was released as part of the company’s Objets Nomades series.

5. Louis Vuitton Tambour Spin Time Regatta White Gold Mens Watch - $70,000

The Louis Vuitton Tambour Spin Time Regatta White Gold is a mans watch that features a red push button to activate the Regatta complication at eight o’clock. Another push of the button and the chrono indication in the 12 o’clock aperture is replaced by Regate. The 30-minute counter will then jump forward and in its place will be a five-minute countdown prominently displayed in red in the counter. The countdown will be indicated by the changing colors of the five cubes on the right part of the dial. The watch measures 45.5 mm in diameter, has a power reserve of 48 hours and is waterproof up to a distance of 100 meters.

4. Louis Vuitton Coquille d’Oeuf Minaudiere Bag - $101,000

The Louis Vuitton Coquille d’Oeuf Minaudiere bag was introduced as part of the spring and summer collection of the company in 2012. The bag honors the partnership between the company and Marc Jacobs, its creative director since 1997. The Jacobs name is a prominent fashion brand in itself. Honored as one of the most influential people in the world and one of the most powerful gay men in the United States, Jacobs has helped steer the artistic vision of the company.

3. Louis Vuitton Tambour Bijou Secret White Gold Women’s Watch - $121,000

The Louis Vuitton Tambour Bijou Secret White Gold is a watch for women. It boasts of 495 diamonds, with 315 on its strap and another 183 in the white gold case. Along with a mother of pearl, the diamonds on the case hides the face of the watch under a Monogram flower. The case measures 18 millimeters in diameter and features a monogram engraving on the rear portion. There is a bespoke steel time adjustment tool.

2. Louis Vuitton Kusama Pumpkin Minaudiere Jewel Bag - $133,400

The Louis Vuitton Kusama Pumpkin Minaudiere Jewel bag was a limited edition bag created by Yayoi Kusama. The bag is made out of pure gold, black resin and some of the finest metal in the world. Inspired by the small evening bags popular in Western Europe in the 1930's, only five pieces were ever made.

1. Louis Vuitton Michael Clarke Luxury Trunk - $170,000

The Louis Vuitton Michael Clarke Luxury Trunk was specially made for the captain of the Australian cricket team. The trunk has wooden frames made out of three-decade old poplar that has been dried for four years. It also has solid brass locks that is said to be pick proof.
