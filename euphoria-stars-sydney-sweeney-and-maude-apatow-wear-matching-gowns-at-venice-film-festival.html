Euphoria Stars Sydney Sweeney And Maude Apatow Wear Matching Gowns At Venice Film Festival

Euphoria stars Sydney Sweeney and Maude Apatow walked the red carpet at the Venice Film Festival on Friday with their stunning gowns.

While attending the premiere of Bones And All at the festival, the co-stars both wore black Giorgio Armani gowns.

The two made individual statements with their chosen glam, which was created using Armani Beauty products.

Sweeney donned a navy satin dress with a large bow, and her makeup was more natural. She pinned her long hair back with another large bow.

Apatow paired her strapless gown with a statement red lip and a side part to match.

According to PEOPLE, Sweeney and Apatow have been inseparable since meeting on the set of the hit HBO series four years ago.

Sweeney said, “Everyone knows that if I'm not in my trailer, I'm in Maude's or if Maude's not in hers, she's in mine,"

"She's the sister I always wished I had."

The two say they typically spend their time in their pajamas, scrolling TikTok or looking for memes to send to one another when they’re together.

According to Sweeney's statement on PEOPLE, "We found relief through the dancing on TikTok.”

"When we got bored, we did some moves. We're very much homebodies. I don't think we're that cool!"

In an Instagram post, Maude posted a picture of them while holding hands. Sydney even commented on Maude’s choice of makeup, writing, “D*mnnnn” with multiple fire emojis on her friend's post.

As for Maude and Sydney, here's hoping the two of them reunite for Euphoria season 3 soon. There's no word on a release date, but new episodes have already been confirmed by Barbie Ferrieira. Unfortunately, not everyone will be coming back to show with her. In an Instagram post earlier this month, the actress announced her departure from the show. As of now, Maude and Sydney are expected to reprise their roles as Lexi and Cassie Howard.
