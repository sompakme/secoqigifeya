Iggy Azalea Wears White Chicks Halloween Costume After Snoop Dogg Diss

Happy Halloween!

A photo posted by Iggy Azalea (@thenewclassic) on Oct 10, 2014 at 10:20pm PDT

Take that Snoop Dogg! Iggy Azalea decided to dress up as Marlon Wayans from White Chicks for Halloween, weeks after the rapper said she looked like the gender-bending character.

"Happy Halloween!" the singer, 24, captioned an Instagram pic of herself on Friday, Oct. 31. Azalea went all out for the look, wearing a denim jacket, pink top, and white gloves. She carried a pink purse and wore a blonde wig.

"Me @aleherself and @priscillaono," she added, posing next to two girlfriends. She also posted a video of herself dancing at a star-studded party. "Bye haterzzzzz," she captioned.

Azalea was such a hit, that even her famous pals were admiring the costume choice. "Me, @jlo & @thenewclassic who had me dying tonight with that costume," Demi Lovato captioned a pic with Azalea and Jennifer Lopez. "Very fun party tonight..#happyhalloween." Lovato went as Wilma Flinstone for the night out, while Lopez donned a sexy latex devil ensemble.
