Niklas Kronwall is a Swedish professional ice hockey player which has an estimated net worth of $5 million.

Niklas Kronwall started playing in the National Hockey League when he was chosen by the Detroit Red Wings during the NHL Entry Draft in 2000. During the 2004-2005 NHL lockout, he did one season stint with Red Wings' affiliate at AHL's Grand Rapids Griffins and received an Eddie Shore Award as one of the top defencemen in the AHL.

He led the team in winning the Stanley Cup in 2008. Kronwall recorded eight goals, 41 assists in 79 games for the Red Wings' 2013-2014 NHL season.

Hans Niklas Kronwall was born on January 12, 1981 in Stockholm, Sweden. His girlfriend, Katinka gave birth to their first son named Douglas on April 24, 2014.
