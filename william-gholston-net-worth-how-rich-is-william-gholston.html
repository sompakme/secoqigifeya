Who Is William Gholston?

William Gholston is a football player who has played as a defenseman for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League (NFL).

The American-born player had a successful career playing college football at Michigan State University, before he got drafted by the Buccaneers as 126th pick, in the fourth round of the 2013 NFL Draft,

Gholston had signed a four-year contract worth $2,560,544; with the team.

His currently NFL statistics shows a total of 401 tackles, 19.5 sacks, 3 forced fumbles, 3 fumble recoveries and 12 pass deflections.

During his rookie season, Gholston earned 30 combined tackles (14 solo) and two quarterback sacks. He completed the 2014 season with 41 combined tackles (27 solo) and two quarterback sacks.

In 2015, Gholston appeared in all 16 games and started 11. He ended with amassed 67 tackles, three sacks and a forced fumble, and in 2016, he recorded 49 tackles, three sacks, and one forced fumble.

In 2017, Gholston’s contract with the Buccaneers was extended for another five years. In 2022 and 2023, his contract with the team was renewed back-to-back again.
