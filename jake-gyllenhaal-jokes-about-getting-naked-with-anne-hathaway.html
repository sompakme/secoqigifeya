A merkin, he joked, is a device that "prostitutes used to wear when they shaved their pubic hair in the 16th century … because they thought they had crabs." He said his device is "a cover for your junk."

"Out of respect to Anne, I’m going to have to leave that up to her when she comes on the show," he said, but then revealed that "it's a similar sort of thing, but it has other functions."
