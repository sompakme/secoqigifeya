Robbie Gould is an American football player who is currently playing for Chicago Bears as a placekicker. His net worth is estimated to be around $22 million.

Robbie Gould' career as a footballer started when he played for the Central Mountain High School. With his outstanding performance, he was recommended by the school's principal to Penn State head football coach Joe Paterno. He was asked to try out as a freshman walk-on the collegiate team.

He had a brief stint with the New England Patriots and Baltimore Ravens during the offseason of NFL in 2005. On the same year, Gould was a undrafted free agent until he joined the Chicago Bears.

Robert Paul Gould III was born on December 6, 1982 in Jersey Shore, Pennsylvania. He is married to Lauren and has a son.
