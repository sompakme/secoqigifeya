Real Housewives Couples Who Divorced After Appearing On TV

Bravo's Real Housewives is no stranger to the drama, especially when it comes to its entertaining, but explosive reunion moments! While the series has remained a success since its debut back in 2006 with the Real Housewives of Orange County, the same cannot be said for some of these housewives' marriages.

Related: How Much Is ‘RHOC’ Star Gretchen Rossi Worth Today?

As fans have peaked into the lives of Teresa Giudice, Ramona Singer, and Erika Jayne, to name a few, they have seen both the highs and lows of their relationships, however, despite their husbands sometimes stealing the show, their marriages were not able to stand the test of time.

10 Erika Jayne & Tom Girardi

Despite appearing like the happy couple they portrayed to be, it seems as if it was all smoke and mirrors. On the most recent season of RHOBH, Erika made it clear that she had planned on staying with Tom till he passes, but that didn't come to be the case!

The duo announced their divorce last November following news that Tom and his firm had embezzled millions. While Erika claims she had no hand in any of this, fans aren't really buying her side of the story, which will continue to unravel this season.

9 Ramona Singer & Mario Singer

Ramona and Mario Singer were all anyone could talk about during the early days of RHONY. The duo had quite the picture-perfect marriage, and after nearly 20 years together, the two were praised for being able to keep the spark alive.

Well, in 2014, Ramona filed for divorce from Mario after discovering in the tabloids that he was cheating on her! Yikes. The news came as quite a shock, but not too much a shock considering the fortune teller in Morocco foreshadowed the entire thing!

8 Teresa Giudice & Joe Giudice

Teresa and Joe Giudice were the talk of the town in New Jersey, however, their 20-year-long marriage came to an end following Joe's release from ICE. After the duo served a collective 4 years behind bars, it was game over on their relationship.

Joe Giudice was deported to Italy after his sentence, making it too difficult to reconcile with Teresa from aboard. Considering all the two had been through, fans weren't the most shocked when Teresa filed for divorce. 

7 Cynthia Bailey & Peter Thomas

Cynthia Bailey and her hubby, Peter Thomas were absolutely couple goals on the Real Housewives of Atlanta. Despite the budding romance, the duo split from one another in 2017 after Cynthia made it clear that she had "reached her breaking point."

Although their separation wasn't easy, both Cynthia and Peter have since found love in other people, making it clear that love really is out there!

6 Danielle Staub & Marty Caffrey

Danielle Staub was notorious for having been engaged not once, not twice, but a whopping 21 times! When the star returned to the series in season 8, she had only been engaged 19 times before tying the knot with Marty Caffrey.

The two only lasted a few months before Danielle was making heinous remarks about Marty's kids, which ultimately destroyed their relationship for good. The divorce took quite some time and even had the two living under the same roof for over a year after, which certainly couldn't have been comfortable.

5 Phaedra Parks & Apollo Nida

Phaedra Parks and Apollo Nida were quite the hot couple on RHOA, however, their love wasn't ht enough to keep them together. When Apollo was sentenced to prison, fans immediately suspected that he and Parks would not make it out of this one alive.

Well, turns out, they were right! Parks' split from Apollo became official in 2016, and they certainly did not end on the most amicable of terms, however, for the sake of their children, the duo have managed to make it work.

4 Adrienne Maloof & Paul Nassif

Adrienne Maloof and Paul Nassif's divorce was one that really shocked Real Housewives fans. The duo was without a doubt admired for their marriage on RHOBH, however, following Adrienne's departure from the series, the couple announced they were officially splitting up.

Brandi Glanville was accused by RHOBH guest, Faye Resnick far causing the split, however, it became clear things were much deeper than that. Adrienne had accused Paul of domestic violence, which took things to a whole other level. Today, the duo have since made things right between them, mainly for the sake of their children.

3 Yolanda Hadid & David Foster

Yolanda Hadid and David Foster had fostered the perfect marriage together! Their life in Malibu appeared to be as bright as their lemon trees, however, everything unraveled following Hadid's Lyme disease diagnosis.

While she has since gotten immensely better, the star claimed that her disease definitely made things hard between her and David. After her departure in season 6, Yolanda and David officially called it quits, which definitely came as a shock to the cast and fans of the show.

2 Camille Meyer & Kelsey Grammer

Camille Meyer, formerly Grammer, was married to Hollywood actor, Kelsey Grammer. The duo share their two kids, which was documented in the very first season of RHOBH. While fans didn't get to see much of Kelsye on the show, it was all most likely because he was out cheating on Camille.

The star was blindsided by Kelsey telling her their marriage was over, which viewers were able to get a peek into come the first season. After tying the knot in 1997, the two split in 2011 after 14 years together.

1 LuAnn de Lesseps & Tom D'Agostino

LuAnn de Lesseps may have been over her head, especially since her friends quite literally told her she was, however, she still went through with her nuptials to Tom D'Agostino. The two remained together from 2016 till their divorce less than a year later in 2017.

Luann revealed that Tom was simply someone who could not be tied down, which just about everyone and their mother knew but Luann!
