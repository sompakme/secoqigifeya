Sebastian Lletget And Becky G Relationship And Dating Life

Sebastian Lletget, an American soccer player, is in a romantic relationship with famous singer Becky G. They have been dating for more than six years.

The relationship between Sebastian and Becky is one of the most popular ones in the US. Their dating life has amassed a lot of media attention due to their celebrity fame.

Lletget is a professional football player who plays midfielder for club FC Dallas of Major League Soccer and the US national team. Whereas Becky G is a popular singer and pop star.

Becky G and Sebastian Lletget may not be in the news daily, but they’re one of our favorite celebrity couples. The 25-year-old singer and actress have been dating the 30-year-old soccer player for the last six years.

Despite their prominence, the couple met in an entirely conventional way: through mutual friends. The relationship between Sebastian and Becky has matured like their age in the last six years, but they have kept their bond brand new.

Sebastian Lletget And Becky G Age Compared

Sebastian Lletget is 30 years old, born on September 3, 1992, in San Francisco, California, United States. 

Similarly, Becky G was born in Inglewood, California, on March 2, 1997, so she is currently 25. The couple has an age gap of five years, and it has not affected their relationship in any way.

At the beginning of their relationship, the singer was 22, and the athlete was 27.

Sebastian Lletget And Becky G Belong To Different Ethnicity

Although it is tough to notice, Lletget and G have different ethnicity as they have separate descent.

Lletget was born to Argentine parents and grew up in South San Francisco, California. He attended South San Francisco High School and played for Santa Clara Sporting of Silicon Valley before joining the United States under-17 residency program.

Scouts from the West Ham United International academy noticed him playing for Santa Clara, and he came to England in 2009.

Becky Gomez was born to Mexican-American parents, Alejandra “Alex” and Francisco “Frank” Gomez. Her four grandparents are from Jalisco, Mexico, while her parents and most of her family were born in the United States.

The player has Argentine ethnicity, while the singer has Mexican descent. Nonetheless, they both hold American nationality as they were born in the US.

Becky G And Sebastian Lletget Met Through Their Friends

Becky G and Sebastian Lletget are among the most prominent couples in America’s entertainment and sports industry. This relationship was possible only because of their mutual friends.

The singer and the player thank their friends for helping build this beautiful bond.

It was a bit of a roundabout connection, according to J-14 in a 2017 radio interview with Mega 96.3. Becky and Naomi Scott became friends while filming Power Rangers, and Naomi’s husband, Jordan Spence, has played soccer with Sebastian.

Naomi and Jordan had the brilliant idea of bringing Becky and Sebastian together, which was, ideally, a perfect one.

It did, however, take some persuasion. Despite her costar’s efforts, Becky said she wasn’t too keen on the idea of being set up on a date. But a video of Sebastian ended up altering her opinion for the better.

She thought that she didn’t want to be with anybody. G had already been there, done that with the relationship thing. She was more focused back then.

First Meeting And First Date

The couple did not take long enough to meet and plan a date.

Her friend was talking to her husband about Sebby, and The singer thought, who’s this Sebby guy? What are they talking about? Then the couple showed her a video of him. Becky then tried to downplay it because she started liking him.

He was good-looking and attractive. Although it came out of nowhere, she started to have a feeling for him. They went on their first date not long after, and Becky later recounted it in an Instagram post congratulating her new lover on a career milestone.

Since then, the pair has been together. Becky stated that Sebastian is more than just her boyfriend; he is her best friend and biggest admirer.

Becky told Entertainment Tonight that she and Sebastian aren’t planning a wedding anytime soon, despite their love for one other and their future.

Of course, they are famous, and it’s cool to hear if that’s how it looks from the outside, but on the inside, it’s just them doing what they love to do with the person they love.

The Covid and homestay orders were a blessing for the couple. Since the government issued the quarantine orders in March, it’s been a blessing for them since they’ve never had the chance to spend as much time as they have.

But the pair were also incredibly compassionate, understanding that it’s not like that for many couples. They knew that many people were struggling and going through real-life situations similar to theirs.

She also revealed the secret of their relationship. One of the biggest things is their friendship.

Both are the most significant admirers of each other and are there as best friends, not only as partners. This has helped them maintain a healthy relationship.

Becky G’s Family Loves Sebastian Lletget

Becky G. recently disclosed to People that her boyfriend, soccer star Sebastian Lletget, has a fantastic bonding with her family.

Sebastian’s Argentine heritage and Becky’s Mexican roots allow the two to connect on a deeper level. “He can converse with my abuelitos,” Becky stated. It’s adorable and pretty lovely. When someone does relate to your culture, it is a different relationship.

Though the “Shower” singer likes dating a soccer player, she has concerns about his connections to Team USA. Becky needed to figure out who to support when the United States recently faced Mexico in the Gold Cup. 

Whatever her opinion on soccer is, it’s evident that Becky is head over heels in love with Sebastian. The pair would be lying if they stated they hadn’t worked extremely hard to reach this stage.

Sebastian Lletget And Becky G Dating Timeline

Sebastian’s statement is much more heartfelt. He practically wrote a love letter on Instagram to his amour.

These were just the beginning of something extraordinary. They have been in a romantic relationship for over six years, and nothing has changed.
