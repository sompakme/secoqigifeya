    FAQs

    Why powdered laundry detergent?

    Powdered laundry detergent offers many benefits. When compared to liquid detergent, it’s more cost-effective as it’s sold in larger quantities. Powders are highly concentrated, which means you can use less product per load, making them a more economical choice as well.

    Powdered laundry detergent is also beneficial in that it has a longer shelf life and usually offers eco-friendly packaging, which makes doing laundry more eco-friendly.
