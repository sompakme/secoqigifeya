Personal Life and Tragic Death

Naya Rivera’s personal life was marked by relationships, motherhood, and ultimately, a tragic death that shocked her fans and the entertainment industry. Rivera had several high-profile relationships throughout her career, capturing the attention of tabloids and fans alike.

One of Rivera’s early relationships was with actor Tahj Mowry, known for his role in the television sitcom “Smart Guy.” The couple dated for a period before parting ways. Rivera also had a relationship with fellow “Glee” cast member Mark Salling, who tragically died by suicide in 2018.

In 2014, Rivera married actor Ryan Dorsey. The couple welcomed their son, Josey Hollis Dorsey, in September 2015. However, their marriage ended in divorce in 2018. Despite the challenges in her personal life, Rivera remained devoted to being a loving mother to her son.

Tragically, on July 8, 2020, Rivera went missing during a boating trip with her son at Lake Piru in California. After her son was found alone on the rented boat, a search and rescue operation was launched. Five days later, Rivera’s body was recovered from the lake. The cause of her death was determined to be accidental drowning. The news of her death sent shockwaves through the entertainment industry, leaving fans mourning the loss of a talented and vibrant artist.
