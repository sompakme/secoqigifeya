See Which Female Stars Traded in Their Red Carpet Dresses for Suits

There is something so exhilarating about seeing our favorite stars ditch their dresses and skirts for pantsuits.

Victoria Beckham, Kendall Jenner, Meryl Streep, Kourtney Kardashian, Evan Rachel Wood and countless other stars have demonstrated the power of the suit and the seemingly infinite creative ways you can wear it.

Whether it’s Princess Kate proving that a royal can carry out her duties just as effectively in a pantsuit as she can in dresses and skirts; Hailey Bieber demonstrating the power of an oversized suit paired with sneakers; or Hailee Steinfeld showing us that a pantsuit can also be worn cropped at the knee, these show stopping looks have prompted Us to take serious style notes.

From plunging necklines to fun fringe detailing and unexpected prints, these iconic looks have Us lusting after more.
