Air Jordan 12 'Light Aqua' To Be Available For Women &amp; Girls

The Air Jordan is once again getting a design upgrade- only this time, it's being tailored toward young girls instead of men,

According to SneakerNews.com, the Air Jordan 12 Grade School Light Aqua is the newest design to come from the always popular sneaker company. With a sharp color palette of white and aqua, it's definitely a change from the normal Air Jordan colors we've grown used to. These sneakers include a white leather upper, with the midsole and leather overlays in the aforementioned Light Aqua. There's also black trim to complete the overall look of the new sneakers.

The Light Aqua color may remind some of Tiffany Blue, another popular color for women. But what's even better about these sneakers is the trademark Zoom Air unit in the midsole, designed to cushion your feet while you wear them. Those that wear Air Jordans while playing sports or working out will especially appreciate this feature, as it'll give their feet a bit of a break while wearing them.

These particular Air Jordans are definitely aimed towards girls, as evident from both the color scheme and sizing that's going to be made available. Nike has the sneakers coming in girls' sizes only and will fit both toddlers and grade-school girls. They're definitely perfect for any young girl who isn't afraid of bright colors and will be fairly active to put them to good use.

The Air Jordan 12 Grade School Light Aqua is expected to be released on Saturday, August 4, 2018, with a retail price of $140 (plus taxes). Air Jordan enthusiasts and casual shoe fans alike can snag a pair online or in-store from either Nike or Foot Locker.
