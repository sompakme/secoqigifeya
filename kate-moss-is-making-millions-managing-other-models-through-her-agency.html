Kate Moss Is Making Millions Managing Other Models Through Her Agency

Highlights

  • Kate Moss' modeling company, the Kate Moss Agency, has earned her £4.3 million by managing fellow models like Rita Ora and Ella Richards.
  • Moss's financial acumen shines through with £831,000 in agency earnings and minimal liabilities of only £13 in bank loans and overdrafts.
  • With an asset base of £5.9 million, including £2.6 million in cash, Moss's entrepreneurial success continues to grow as she manages talents like her daughter Lila.

Kate Moss owes much of her $70 million net worth to her successful modeling career, but she’s passionate about helping others get in the business, which has apparently given Moss millions in profits.

Recent financial reports reveal that Kate has reportedly made £4.3 million through her management of fellow models, including her daughter Lila and singer Rita Ora, through her modeling company, the Kate Moss Agency, which she launched in 2016.

Kate’s company has experienced remarkable growth, representing talents like Ella Richards, granddaughter of Rolling Stones' Keith, and model Thomas Eblen, known as Blondey McCoy.

The agency's performance is evident in its latest figures, boasting £831,000 in earnings for the year ending July 31, 2023. Moss, serving as the firm's director, demonstrated astute financial management, with minimal liabilities of only £13 in bank loans and overdrafts.

With a substantial asset base of £5.9 million (including £2.6 million in cash) Moss's entrepreneurial prowess continues to shine, marking a significant increase in net assets from £3.5 million in July 2022 to £4.31 million.

“Kate started the business at the time Lila was starting to model,” a source told The U.S. Sun about Kate’s success. “It was also at the time Kate herself was changing her lifestyle and stopping her renowned partying.”

“So it has been a roaring success and she has other high-profile stars, like Rita, on the books,” they continued. "Kate has so much experience in the industry and has been able to apply it to what she knows best — modelling.”

Beginning her career in the late 1980s, Moss quickly became a symbol of the fashion industry's "waif" look, gracing the covers of countless magazines and walking the runway for renowned designers. Her unique blend of edgy allure and ethereal beauty captivated audiences worldwide, earning her lucrative contracts with top brands like Calvin Klein, Chanel, and Burberry.

Over the years, the model diversified her portfolio by venturing into various business endeavors, including fragrance lines, collaborations with fashion brands, and even music. However, her most notable entrepreneurial venture came in 2016 with the launch of the Kate Moss Agency. Leveraging her extensive industry experience and connections, Moss aimed to provide a platform for aspiring models while also managing established talents.

Now, Moss continues to model while managing many other famous faces in the industry, including her look-a-like daughter Lila, who has already established herself as a well-rounded model in the fashion industry.
