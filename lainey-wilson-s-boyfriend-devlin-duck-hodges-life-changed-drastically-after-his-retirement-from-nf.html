What Happened To Lainey Wilson's Boyfriend, Devlin Duck Hodges' After He Retired From the NFL

According to Life & Style Mag, Devlin Duck Hodges experienced a significant transformation following his retirement from the NFL. Initially making a name for himself at Samford University, Hodges entered the NFL as an undrafted free agent, signing with the Pittsburgh Steelers in 2019. His career also included stints with the Los Angeles Rams and the Ottawa Redblacks of the CFL.

Post-retirement, Hodges ventured into new territories, channeling his lifelong passion for the outdoors into a podcast centered on duck hunting, aptly named the "Duck Hodges Podcast."

This podcast reflects Hodges' expertise and enthusiasm for hunting and wildlife, an interest he has nurtured since his childhood. His skills in duck hunting have been well recognized, including winning the 2018 Alabama State Duck Calling Championship Duck Caller title and qualifying several times for the World Duck Calling Contest.

Since retiring from professional football, Hodges has embarked on a new career in real estate, joining the team at Mossy Oak Properties in Louisiana in November 2022. At Mossy Oak Properties, Hodges focused on assisting clients with hunting camps, hunting land, farmland, and other real estate needs.
