Brandi Rhodes: All Elite Wrestling Is Totally Inclusive

All Elite Wrestling’s signing of Sonny Kiss and Nyla Rose was largely celebrated, but has been met with some backlash from homophobic parts of the internet. Earlier today, All Elite Wrestling was asked to address the darker side of their fanbase publicly. Brandi Rhodes did just that on her official Twitter account.

“As we look ahead to Las Vegas, let’s take this opportunity to make it clear that AEW is totally inclusive,” said Rhodes. “We fully support and celebrate all of our athletes, and all of our fans, regardless of their sexual orientation, race, gender, religion or ethnicity. Period. A safe, inclusive, respectful and very cool environment will be central to everything we do at AEW. Be who you are, and come as you are. Because we’re all going to come together as a community to change the world.”
