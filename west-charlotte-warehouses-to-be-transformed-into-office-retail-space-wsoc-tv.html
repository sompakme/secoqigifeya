West Charlotte warehouses to be transformed into office, retail space WSOC TV

CHARLOTTE, N.C. — An Atlanta developer that acquired several properties in Charlotte’s Seversville and Wesley Heights neighborhoods earlier this year has unveiled plans for its holdings.

Third & Urban said Tuesday it would upfit multiple properties along Tuckaseegee Road and Gesco and Jay streets into 260,000 square feet of office, showroom and retail space. The sites Third & Urban acquired in multiple transactions in the past several months contain several midcentury warehouses, including a former Clorox manufacturing center.

The properties are at 929 and 1018 Jay St., 700 Tuckaseegee Road and 800 Gesco St.
