His name is often linked with songs such as “Wichita Lineman,” “By the Time I Get to Phoenix,” and “Rhinestone Cowboy,” among others. Glen Campbell was not just a well-known singer, but he was also a well-known television presenter and, on occasion, an actor. He died away in August of this year.
