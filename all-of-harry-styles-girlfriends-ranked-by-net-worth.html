All Of Harry Styles Girlfriends, Ranked By Net Worth

  • Olivia Wilde ($25 million)

  • Taylor Swift ($570 million)

After auditioning for X Factor in 2010, Harry Styles was paired with four young individuals who helped form the band One Direction. After participating in the show and reaching the runners-up position, the boy band became a worldwide phenomenon, making Styles a heartthrob. After experiencing success in a band, he embarked on a solo career in 2017, earning him greater success. Through his sold-out tours, music, and brand endorsements, Style has amassed a massive $100 million fortune. Along with his professional career, the singer has been under media focus for his relationships with famous personalities.

Victoria’s Secret models to dating singers Harry Styles has dated a series of A-list celebrities over the years and has been in a steady relationship since 2021. From his high-profile romance with Taylor Swift to dating Olivia Wilde, let’s take a look at all of Harry Styles’ girlfriends, ranked by their net worth.

Nadine Leopold ($600,000)

Harry Styles and Nadine Leopold began their relationship in 2014, and during that time, they were spotted going out on several dates. According to Glamor Magazine, the couple had a steady romance for a year but ended things in March 2015. Leopold hails from Austria and is best known for walking at the 2017 and 2018 Victoria’s Secret Show. She has also posed for issues of Cosmopolitan and Vogue.

Georgia Fowler ($1 million)

Georgia Fowler was only sixteen when she was signed onto IMG Models to begin her career in fashion. Along with being a regular on Victoria’s Secret shoots, she has walked the brand's runway from 2016 to 2018. The model dated Harry Styles for a short period during the end of 2015. She also posted a video of him playing Scrabble with her.

Emily Atack ($1.5 million)

Emily Atack and Harry Styles were rumored to be dating in 2012, but it wasn’t until 2014 that Atack confirmed the news, stating that it was a casual relationship and the couple parted ways on mutual terms. The actress became prominent after starring in Dad’s Army, has also worked in Almost Married, Get Lucky, and now hosts The Emily Atack Show.

Tess Ward ($1.5 million)

After developing a taste for cooking during her university years, Tess Ward began her journey after her travels to India. Ward is best known for her cookbook titled The Naked Diet. She also has a popular food blog with a large audience. Harry Styles and Tess Ward met via a mutual friend in 2017. They maintained their relationship under wraps before Ward broke up to reconcile with her ex-boyfriend, as noted by Page Six.

Erin Foster ($2 million)

During a brief time in 2014, Harry Styles and Erin Foster were spotted hanging out together. The couple began dating rumors after they enjoyed a day at the pumpkin patch during the Halloween season that year. The daughter of music legend David Foster, Erin has ventured into cinema and serves as a producer for various projects, including He’s Such A Girl and House.

Camille Rowe ($6 million)

Camille Rowe was discovered in 18 when studying in Paris. Along with being Victoria’s Secret model, she has also walked the runway for various brands such as Tommy Hilfiger, Gap, and Dior. Camille and Harry famously dated for a year between 2017 and 2018. The singer wrote Cherry about her from the album Fine Line and even added her voicemail to the end of the song.

Sara Sampaio ($10 million)

Harry Styles dated Sara Sampaio in 2015, and they were first spotted together in New York when the couple had a date night. Their relationship didn’t last for long, and they soon ended things. Sampaio was born in Portugal and has been the beauty ambassador for Giorgio Armani. She was the first Portuguese model to feature in Sports Illustrated magazine in 2014.

Olivia Wilde ($25 million)

Director/actress Olivia Wilde first gained recognition starring as Thirteen in House MD. After starring in several films, she made her directorial debut with Booksmart in 2019. During the filming of her second movie, Don’t Worry Darling, the couple met when Styles was cast as the lead. As mentioned by Elle, they were first spotted together at a wedding ceremony in 2021 and had been going steady.

Kendall Jenner ($45 million)

Kendall Jenner and Harry Styles had an on-again-off-again relationship that lasted several years between their other romances. They were first spotted together in November 2013 before breaking up in February 2014. A year later, they rekindled their romance with a romantic yacht trip during the New Year. The couple parted ways again in 2016 but remain good friends.

Taylor Swift ($570 million)

With ten albums released and two re-released, Taylor Swift has been a phenomenon around the world since her debut album in 2006. When Harry Styles and Taylor Swift began their short-lived relationship in 2012, they were considered a power couple on the rise. Since their breakup, Swift has written Out Of The Woods about him while Styles penned Two Ghosts for her, as stated by Seventeen.

Harry Styles has tried to maintain the privacy of his relationships over the years and talks as little about them in public as possible. The singer has been in a relationship with Olivia Wilde since 2021, and the couple starred in Wilde’s directed film Don’t Worry Darling in 2022, which premiered at the Venice Film Festival.
