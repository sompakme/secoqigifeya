What Happened Between Kate Hudson And Nick Jonas?

Fabletics' co-owner Kate Hudson and singer-songwriter Nick Jonas were rumored to be dating late in September 2015 as revealed by Us Weekly.It was also revealed that Kate Hudson's brother, Oliver, was the one who introduced the two. The next day, the pair were seen eating brunch together in Miami. They seemed to have great connection, but what really happened between Nick Jonas and Kate Hudson?

Nick Jonas' Unbelievable Connection With Kate Hudson

When the dating rumors first made headlines in the tabloids, the pair initially kept mum on their supposed relationship and didn't address the issue at all.

Kate Hudson went to Watch What Happens: Live with Andy Cohen at the time when rumors first circulated, so it's inevitable that the host would ask her about Nick Jonas however she declined to comment and said, “I don’t like addressing any kind of gossip.”

While the actress was adamant on keeping things private, Nick Jonas was pretty open about their incredible connection.

When asked about what he thought about the actress he said, “Kate’s incredible. We had an unbelievable connection as two humans who just admire things about each other, and see something in each other that’s beautiful."

Despite confirming that they had an intimate bond together, Nick Jonas declined to comment further on their status out of respect for Kate Hudson's privacy.

Upon hearing about what Nick Jonas has to say, Kate Hudson was pretty pleased and said favorable words about Nick. She said during her interview with Gayle King, “He’s a great guy. Apparently that we have a beautiful connection!”

The Budding Relationship Between Kate Hudson And Nick Jonas Eventually Fizzled

While the pair didn't officially become a couple, they did spend some time together. The brief fling between the two might not have blossomed into something more, but the pair admittedly retained their mutual respect for each other.

The Bride Wars star admitted that she's just really attracted to people who are musically inclined. The actress who had a string of musician exes never denied her attraction to guys with rock star vibes.

She said, “I’m attracted to musical people, period. From the outside, yeah, I get it. ‘Oh, I like a rock star,’ or whatever. But that’s not really what it is. The reason I connect so deeply with musicians … is because we all connect to music in a way that you don’t have to explain. You just feel it, and it’s something you love. The lifestyle is not something to fall in love with.”

Eight years after their supposed fling, both Kate and Nick have now settled down with their long term partners. Kate Hudson and musician Danny Fujikawa started dating in 2016 and then welcomed their first child together in 2018.
