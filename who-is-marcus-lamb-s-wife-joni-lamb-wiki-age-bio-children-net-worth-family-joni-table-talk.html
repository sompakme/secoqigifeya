Joni Lamb Biography – Joni Lamb Wiki

Is a Christian broadcaster, television executive, producer, singer, author, and talk show host. She is the wife of Marcus Lamb, an American televangelist, minister, prosperity theologian, and Christian broadcaster. Her husband, Marcus Lamb died on November 30, 2021, from COVID-19 complications. The Greenville, South Carolina native grew up attending the Tremont Avenue Church of God with her family.

Joni and her husband, Marcus co-founded the Daystar Television Network and have worked together for more than three decades broadcasting the Christian Gospel through television. Their Daystar Television Network is among the largest Christian television networks in the world and reaches more than 108 million homes in the United States overall major cable carriers and satellite systems.

She is the Executive Producer and host of her daily talk show, “Joni Table Talk,” the show addresses relevant, Godly solutions for today’s complex problems. As of 2021, Joni Lamb’s “Joni Table Talk” has received ten Telly Awards, three Lone Star Emmy Awards, six Lone Star Emmy Nominations, and five Daytime Emmy Nominations. Together with her late husband, Marcus, they hosted “Marcus & Joni” a Daystar’s daily, live, flagship broadcast. The show was recently known as “Ministry Now!”
