Louis Koo Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Louis Koo (Koo Tin-lok???) is a Hong Kong film actor who was born on October 21, 1970. He started his career as an actor in local television series, earning the TVB Best Actor award in 1999 and 2001. He has mainly concentrated on his cinematic career over the last decade. Koo has established himself as a veteran of the Hong Kong film industry, serving as a spokesperson for Pepsi, Osim, Tag Heuer, Lotte, Zero Eyewear, Lay’s, and Samsung Galaxy. In 2013, Koo was Hong Kong’s highest-paid actor, earning 236 million HKD (30.4 million USD).
