    • Duke’s first marriage was to Harry Falk, a director. They got married in 1965 but divorced in 1969.

    In addition to her acting achievements, Patty Duke was an advocate for mental health awareness. She openly shared her struggles with bipolar disorder and worked tirelessly to reduce the stigma surrounding mental health issues.

    Her son, Sean Astin, continues to carry on her legacy. In honor of his mother, he established the Patty Duke Mental Health Initiative, which aims to provide support and resources for individuals and families affected by mental health challenges.
