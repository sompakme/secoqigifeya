Aiden Gallagher Parents: Meet Rob Gallagher And Lauren Gallagher

Aidan Gallagher is an American actor.

Gallagher’s first major role was portraying one of the quadruplets, Nicky Harper, in the Nickelodeon comedy television series Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn.

Gallagher’s breakthrough role which has brought him widespread recognition is his portrayal of Number Five in the Netflix series The Umbrella Academy, which began airing in 2019.

Gallagher was born on September 18, 2003 to parents Lauren and Rob.

Gallagher has not revealed much about his parents but they have appeared in pictures on his Instagram account.

That notwithstanding, Gallagher’s parents are very proud of the acting career he has had thus far and as they years roll by, they’ll be hoping that he becomes a bigger star.

In 2018, Gallagher was listed by Variety as a top actor under the age of 25.

Gallagher has been nominated for the Favorite Male TV Star Kids Choice Award twice, and has hosted the Kids Choice Sports Awards.

Gallagher was listed as #2 on IMDB’s Top 10 Breakout Stars of 2020 and #7 on IMDB’s Top 10 Stars of 2020.
