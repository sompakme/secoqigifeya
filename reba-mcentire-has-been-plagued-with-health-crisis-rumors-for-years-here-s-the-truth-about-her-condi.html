Reba McEntire Has Been Plagued With Health Crisis Rumors For Years, Here's The Truth About Her Condi

Highlights

  • Reba McEntire deals with a disc issue in her back.
  • While there are rumors about her health, Reba McEntire maintains a positive attitude.
  • Reba McEntire's commitment to a healthy lifestyle is evident in her book, "Not That Fancy".

Thanks to her beloved presence in the country music scene, it makes perfect sense that Reba McEntire was one of the stars performing at the 2024 Super Bowl. The star took the stage with Tiësto, Andra Day, and Post Malone.

Fans are forever curious about Reba McEntire's life, from why the successful sitcom Reba was canceled to co-star Melissa Peterman's thoughts about her. Fans who have followed her long career have likely read that the singer has cancer as there are many videos and articles suggesting this. However, these are only rumors, and she has never said that this is true. However, the country star does have a health condition that she deals with regularly.

Does Reba McEntire Suffer From Health Issues?

From time to time, Reba McEntire talks about her health in interviews, and she always seems happy to discuss the topic. In a 2017 interview with The Tennessean, Reba McEntire said that she has a disc that she has to keep an eye on. She said:

"I’ve got a disc that kind of comes in and out, so I have to keep my core strength up so I can make it stay in. So I do Pilates and I get on the floor and I go to a physical therapist, and he has shown me how to strengthen my core and to keep me in line and in better health."

Reba McEntire has also talked about arthritis. As she told The Tennessean, her mother was 91 years old at the time of the interview, and she hopes to live past that age as well. McEntire's mother, Jacqueline, passed away at 93in March 2020, according to People.

McEntire said:

“I’m trying to find all the natural remedies to make the arthritis go away and make me feel better so I can live as long as she has.”

Throughout her career, there have been times when the singer has had to focus on her health. In November 2022, according to Hello! Magazine, McEntire postponed three concert dates. She explained that her doctor said she needed to focus on "vocal rest." While it doesn't sound like anything serious was going on, her fans were definitely concerned. As musicians would all probably agree, going on vocal rest is a common occurrence, especially when someone has been singing for decades.

While Reba McEntire doesn't appear to have any serious health problems, there is often news coverage related to how she is feeling. In 2021, McEntire shared that she had COVID-19. According to People, McEntire then explained that she suffered from RSV, not COVID.

While the singer and actress is doing well today, there continue to be rumors about her health. In 2019, the National Enquirer wrote a story saying that she has a problem with her spine. However, McEntire has never confirmed this or commented on this rumor.

While it can't be easy to go on vocal rest or to pay attention to a disc, McEntire maintains a positive attitude when it comes to her health.

Does Reba McEntire Live A Healthy Lifestyle?

Reba McEntire definitely focuses on eating well and exercising, which must be helpful for arthritis and the disc in her body that she deals with. As she explained in an interview with First For Women, she likes walking with Riddler, her French bulldog, and her boyfriend Rex Linn. She makes walking a social activity and invites relatives and friends along, too.

McEntire told First For Women:

"At our age, we’ve got to keep moving. Mama always said, ‘If you don’t use it, you lose it.’ And that’s with your voice, that’s with your muscles. Arthritis is a big thing in my age group, so I keep moving. It’s important."

In 2015, the singer was interviewed by WebMD about her healthy habits and she explained that she focuses on a good diet. She said, “And I try to stay away from sugar. I eat fruits and vegetables. I love salmon for my omega-3s. … take supplements. I try to drink coconut water, which I love, and aloe vera juice."

Reba McEntire seems to love sharing how healthy she is. She talked to Celebrity Sweat about her diet and workout plan and said she likes to exercise in the morning and also make a shake. As for her "cheat meal," she said, "Beans and cornbread, fried potatoes, iced tea." It's relatable to hear that she still indulges in some fun food sometimes.

In fact, McEntire is so devoted to her healthy lifestyle that she wrote a book called "Not That Fancy: Simple Lessons on Living, Loving, Eating and Dusting Off Your Boots," which was published in 2023. While she shared some advice in the book, she also included recipes.
