These Couples From Survivor Are Still Together Today

Survivor first premiered all the way back in 2000 and has been on for 41 seasons now, which is a remarkable run for a show. Over the years that the show has been on, there have been many players who have come and gone trying to be the last one standing, the sole survivor. While many went on the show trying to win, others wound up finding love instead.

There have been so many contestants who have found love on the show at the most unexpected times. While some of these Survivor couples weren't able to survive outside of the show, many of them did, and they even went on to get married and have children, proving that true love can survive.

8 Sierra Dawn Thomas And Joe Anglim

Sierra Dawn Thomas and Joe Anglim first met back in 2014 on Survivor: World Apart. Their relationship didn't start until after the show, and Sierra clearly didn't show Joe any mercy as she voted him off the show.

RELATED: Former Survivor Contestants Reveal The Secret To Getting On The Show

They reconnected once it was over and the two started dating. The two eventually got married in 2019 and made the exciting announcement the following year that they were expecting their first child together. Their daughter, Della Dawn, was born June 2021 and the three are one happy family.

7 Kim Powers And Alex Bell

Kim Powers was on Survivor: Africa back in 2001, and Alex Bell competed on Survivor: The Amazon in 2003. Clearly, the two weren't on the same season of Survivor, but that didn't stop the two from forming a budding romance. They met because of their Survivor connection, and the two wound up getting together. They dated for a few years before they got married in 2006. They also adopted a daughter, Bella. The two are still happily married today and are the happiest little family.

6 Jenna Bowman And Sebastian Noel

Jenna Bowman and Sebastian Noel were both on Survivor: Ghost Island. The two were constantly flirting while on the show, and many even questioned if their budding romance was legit or just for the game. It turns out that their feelings were very real as they're still together today. After the show they proved to the world that they were serious when Jenna moved to Florida to be with Sebastian. The two are big on social media and even have a YouTube channel where they vlog and post videos together.

5 Tyson Apostol And Rachel Foulger

Tyson Apostol is perhaps one of the greatest players to ever compete on Survivor. So far he has competed a total of four times, and managed to win once. Tyson and Rachel Foulger appeared on Survivor: Blood vs. Water together, which was his third time on the show. He was already dating Rachel prior to going on the show, but fans quickly made them a power couple.

When Rachel was voted off the island, Tyson used his anger as fuel to make it all the way to the end and win the game. The two got married back in 2015 and their wedding was officiated by fellow castaway Stephen Fishbach. Tyson and Rachel are still happily married today with two adorable kids.

4 Jaime Dugan-Huffman And Erik Huffman

Jamie Dugan and Erik Huffman first met back in 2007 on Survivor: China. The two quickly bonded on the show, but it wasn't until afterward that their relationship blossomed. They admitted that they had feelings for one another while on the island, but both of them were too focused on the game to even consider having a relationship while competing. Just about a year after dating, the two were engaged, and evntually tied the knot in 2009. The two have a son together and a very much happy together.

3 Elizabeth Beisel And Jack Nichting

Elizabeth Beisel and Jack Nichting are an adorable couple that are still very much happily together after meeting on season 39 of Survivor, Island of the Idols. While neither one of them won their season, they did win in the love department. Like many other Survivor couples, these two waited until after the show to start any sort of romance. The two are still happily together today and you can keep up with them on their social meda.

2 Keith Tollefson And Whitney Duncan

WORDS

Keith Tollefson and Whitney Duncan first met in 2011 on Survivor: South Pacific and The Amazing Race not long afterward. The two dated for a while before they eventually tied the knot in 2014. Recently, Keith and Whitney shared their troubles with getting pregnant, and that they were trying in-vitro fertilization, but still struggled with infertility. Although the two might be going through a tough time while trying to start a family, they're still happily married and in love today.

1 Amber Brkich And Boston Rob Mariano

WORDS

Amber Brkich and Boston Rob Mariano are one of the most famous Survivor couples to date. They first met on season eight, Survivor: All-Stars where they went to the final two together. During the finale, Rob proposed to Amber, and of course, she said yes. The two got married in the Bahamas in 2005 and even had their wedding aired on CBS. The fan-favorite couple have four daughters together, and are still happily married today, making them one of Survivor's greatest romances.
