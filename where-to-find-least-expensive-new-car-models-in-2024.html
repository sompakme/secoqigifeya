2024 Nissan Versa: America’s Least Expensive New Car

The 2024 Nissan Versa has been recognized as America’s least expensive new car. With a starting price of $16,130, the Versa offers great value for money. It comes with a 5-speed manual transmission in the base model, but higher trim levels have a continuously variable automatic transmission. The Versa also boasts features like smartphone integration, split-folding rear seats, and aluminum alloy wheels.

2024 Mitsubishi Mirage: A Budget-Friendly Option

The 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage is another affordable new car option, starting at $16,695. This budget-friendly vehicle offers seating for five, making it a practical choice for small families or those who frequently carpool. What sets the Mirage apart is its excellent fuel efficiency, delivering an impressive 39 mpg combined. This means you can enjoy more miles on the road and spend less time and money at the gas pump.

But affordability doesn’t mean compromising on peace of mind. The 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage comes with a 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain warranty, providing reassurance and protection for years to come. Whether you’re planning long road trips or simply commuting to work, this comprehensive warranty ensures that you’re covered should any unexpected issues arise.

If you prefer a sedan-style vehicle, Mitsubishi also offers the Mirage G4 sedan variant. Starting at $17,795, the Mirage G4 provides more rear-seat legroom compared to its hatchback counterpart, making it a comfortable choice for passengers in the back. The Mirage G4 still retains all the budget-friendly benefits of the standard Mirage, including its fuel efficiency and warranty coverage.

Key Features of the 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage

  • Seating for five
  • Excellent fuel efficiency of 39 mpg combined
  • 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain warranty
  • Spacious rear-seat legroom in Mirage G4 sedan variant

The 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage is a great choice for those seeking an affordable and practical new car. With its exceptional fuel efficiency and comprehensive warranty coverage, it offers both cost savings and peace of mind. Whether you opt for the hatchback or the Mirage G4 sedan, you can enjoy an economical and reliable vehicle that fits your budget.
