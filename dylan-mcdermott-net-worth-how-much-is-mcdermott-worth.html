Early Life and Career Beginnings

Dylan McDermott, born as Mark Anthony McDermott, grew up in New York City. McDermott started his acting career in the 1980s with roles in films like “Hamburger Hill” and “Steel Magnolias.” His breakthrough role came in 1993 with the film “In the Line of Fire,” which cemented his place in the industry.
