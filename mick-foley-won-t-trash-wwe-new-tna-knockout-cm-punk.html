Mick Foley Wont Trash WWE, New TNA Knockout?; CM Punk

–CM Punk will be the host of the "Ultimate Men’s Fitness" event in his hometown of Chicago, Illinois on June 20th.

–Mick Foley updated his blog on his official MySpace account, talking about the content of his latest memoir he is writing with the benefits going to charity. In regards to fans looking for Foley to trash WWE, he stated "I don’t want to kill any enthusiasm for the new book, but those looking for me to blast WWE will likely be disappointed. My feelings for the company, and for Vince himself, are largely positive, even if I am very happy not to be there anymore."

–Speaking of Foley, he has been praising and slipping in references to indy female wrestler Trina Michaels in recent promos. That should lead to some rumors about Michaels joining TNA Wrestling.
