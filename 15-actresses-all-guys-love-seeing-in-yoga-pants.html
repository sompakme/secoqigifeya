Eva Longoria

After getting her start on soap opera The Young and the Restless in 2001 it was just a short time before the world noticed petite star, Eva Longoria. Several publications have referred to the actress, producer, businesswoman, and activist as one of the most beautiful people in the world and because of this, she is part of a few worldwide advertising campaigns for large corporations.

Before becoming a famous actress, Longoria was born to a Mexican-American family in Texas where she studied and had a job at a fast food chain before getting her bachelor's degree in Kinesiology from Texas A&M University - Kingsville.

While in college, Longoria won a pageant which is how she found her way to Los Angeles to be discovered by a talent agent. The drop dead gorgeous star was quickly scooped up to become one of the hottest leading ladies on primetime in Desperate Housewives.

Olivia Wilde

This actress is best known for playing popular character “Thirteen” on now finished television show House. She has starred in tons of television and film before and after her role on House.

She’s not only a hottie, she’s a good person, and that makes us big fans.

Blake Lively

After immediate success on the CW’s teen drama, Gossip Girl Lively easily transitioned into the film industry with relevant parts like her most recent role in The Shallows (2016). While filming Green Lantern in 2010 Lively met Ryan Reynolds and the pair announced that they were dating in 2011. They bought a New York home together in Summer 2012 and were married that Fall. Reynolds was recently trending for describing the immense love that he has for his blonde bombshell wife.

They have two daughters together and although both are still working in the acting world Lively has dreams of being an interior decorator and professional homemaker.

When she posted the photo on Instagram people were pissed and Hudgens ended up paying a $1000 fine to the National Forest Service. Hudgens has focused much more on singing and recording than acting since her High School Musical days and has released two full studio albums since 2006.

Hudgens continues to focus on the entertainment industry and we’re sure we’ll see more of her in the future.
