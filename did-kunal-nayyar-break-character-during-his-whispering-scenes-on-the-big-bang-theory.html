Did Kunal Nayyar Break Character During His 'Whispering' Scenes On The Big Bang Theory?

Before Kunal Nayyar fell into everyone's lives as their favorite quirky immigrant on theBig Bang Theory, he was quite the sensible young actor from India.But then, Kunal moved from India to Portland, Oregon. He studied business. However, the actor always wanted to be an actor. So chose to major in business to have something to fall back. Naturally, this is an intelligent thing to do as acting careers can be turbulent for newcomers.

After earning his degree in business, Kunal went to Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, to make a master's degree in Fine Arts at Temple University.

He also worked for the Royal Shakespeare Company in the United Kingdom. After graduating in 2007, Kunal landed his iconic role on The Big Bang Theory as Dr. Raj Koothrappali.

The Truth About Kunal Nayyar Staying In Character As Raj

There are many tales of actors not staying in character, improvising scenes, or wholly going off script, for bad or worse. Because everything was written so specifically, Kunal admits that it was not the best idea to do much improvisation. Breaking character, however, is something Kunal admits he did all the time. What was the culprit?

Kunal explains that the contagious response of the audience's laughter would impact him. He explains that once the audience would give a significant reaction to one of his jokes, he could not help but join in and laugh as well. Who could forget when Jim Parsons broke character in a scene with Kunal Nayyar?

Since the show was completed in 2019, Kunal announced that he would take a hiatus from work. He told the event as the Summer of Kunal on social media from the South of France. The actor is very connected with his fans. On Instagram, he discusses the personal aspects of his travels and shares "homework" with them. He encourages them to check in with themselves and make sure they're feeding their minds with good food.

Did Kunal Nayyar Break When He Would Whisper To The Others On Big Bang Theory?

During his interview with Oh No They Didn't, Nayyar revealed that he in fact, would not break when whispering during scenes. This would typically take place when wasn't able to talk with women. However, according to Kunal, he was sticking to the script given how little time they had to shoot.

He said, "I don't -- our tapings are so tight, we have it down to a real science. Plus, a lot of the language on the show is so specific that if you change a word it will mess up the whole speech rhythm. But if we're talking about breaking, I break all the time."

A few fans might be heartbroken by this revelation.

What's Next for Kunal?

Kunal Nayyar has not been short of work since The Big Bang Theory ended in 2019. One project the actor has in post-production is a Netflix science-fiction comedy called Spaceman. Adam Sandler helms spaceman. Batman's Golden Globe nominee Paul Dano, A Promising Young Woman star, and two-time Oscar nominee, Carey Mulligan are also a part of the cast.

The film follows the recent Hollywood Trend of adapting films from existing intellectual property. The film is set to adapt the 2017 novel The Spaceman of Bohemia by Jaroslav Kalfar. The novel is about an explorer who departs on an independent space mission into unknown regions. The production has a star-studded team that includes Channing Tatum, Peter Kiernan, Reid Carolin, and Michael Parets. In addition, the film gained Johan Renck as a director. John Renck also directed the hit Chernobyl Series.

Adam Sandler will play the lead role of the independent explorer seeking answers to an ancient mystery. The hero's life on earth is in shambles, and his only guide is somewhat otherworldly. It is unclear what Kunal's role will be, but at least his 29 million Instagram followers are ready for more of him on their screens.
