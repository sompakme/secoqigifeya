Shania Twain Referred To Taylor Swift's Life As An &quot;Exhausting Existence&quot;

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift's life was described as an "exhausting existence" by Shania Twain, despite mutual respect.
  • AJ McLean praised Swift as one of the most grounded, humble performers with no dark side.
  • Taylor Swift showed immense respect for Shania Twain, considering her the reason she pursued music.

Experienced artists like Shania Twain have spoken glowingly about Taylor Swift. That also includes Alanis Morissette, who had the opposite experience with Taylor Swift than what she expected. Morissette quickly learned that Taylor was different, and a joy to be around.

However, Twain did admit that living Taylor Swift's life really isn't easy. She recently revealed the dark side to Swift's fame. We'll take a closer look at Shania's comments, and why she called Swift's life, "an exhausting existence."

Shania Twain Had Plenty Of Praise For Taylor Swift, But Called Her Life "An Exhausting Existence"

Both Shania Twain and Taylor Swift share a mutual respect for one another. During an interview with Extra, Twain applauded Swift for being a 'fabulous example' for young artists. However, Twain also commented on Swift's schedule. Like so many others, Twain admitted such a task can be very draining.

Twain revealed, “She is living an exhausting existence, but she’s dedicated and committed to herself, to her art, and her work,” Twain said. “It isn’t even about ambition, though I’m sure that most people would call her ambitious.”

Still, Twain had a huge amount of respect for Swift and the way she continues to handle herself.

“I know that she’s doing the same in her own right for other young artists coming up behind her, because she is a very brave artist and a very, very hard working one."

Swift herself has always shown the same respect towards Twain.

Taylor revealed in the past, “I remember when I was a little kid, I used to see Shania Twain and I just remember thinking if I ever got a chance, by some crazy streak of luck to be on a stage, I’d want to do as much as I could to say thank you,” Swift said.

Swift went on to reveal she was blown away when that very moment finally took place of meeting her idol.

“Shania Twain walked up and introduced herself to me. Shania Twain, as in the reason I wanted to do this in the first place. Shania Twain, as in the most impressive and independent and confident and successful female artist ever to hit country music.”

Swift adds, “She walked up to me and said she wanted to meet me and tell me I was doing a great job. She was so beautiful, guys. She really is that beautiful. All the while, I was completely star struck.”

A great respect between the two, but in truth, the respect for Taylor Swift seems to be endless these days.

AJ McLean Was Another Artist That Recently Had Major Praise For Taylor Swift

We can add Backstreet Boys star AJ McLean to the long list of artists to praise Taylor Swift. We've seen this in the past, celebs praising Swift for the way she treated their child. The same held true for McLean.

"She is, by far, in my opinion, one of the most grounded, humble performers ever," McLean continued of Swift. "It's like, 'OK, something's gotta be off about you. There's gotta be like a dark side.' And there isn't. She's just... she's met my oldest three times," he said.

"And the last time she met her, she remembered her name, which that, as a dad alone, catapulted her into the stratosphere for me."

"You know, we were backstage. She came back... She's like, 'Ava.' And I was just like, 'What? Mind blown,'" McLean went on, before talking about the name change."

A surreal moment without a doubt.
