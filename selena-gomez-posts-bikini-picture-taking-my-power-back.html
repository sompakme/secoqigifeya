Selena Gomez Posts Bikini Picture: &quot;Taking My Power Back&quot;

Selena Gomez is feeling feisty! The 21-year-old singer posted a stunning bikini selfie via Instagram Wednesday, May 21—and she's brimming with girl power.

The "Come & Get It" hitmaker, who's had a tumultuous start to her year with a cancelled tour and a stint in rehab, not to mention reconnecting with ex-boyfriend Justin Bieber, seems ready to put all the drama behind her.

Gomez captioned the pic, "Taking my power back. Can't wait to show you where I've been. I love y'all. Ps, I still obsess over pickles."

As Us Weekly perviously reported, Gomez's rep described her February stint in rehab as a chance for the singer to "take some time for herself."
