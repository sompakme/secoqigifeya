A Look Inside Stranger Things Stars Natalia Dyer And Charlie Heaton's Romantic Relationship Off-Scre

Stranger Things has been around since 2016. This thrilling drama series quickly becomes a Top 10 on the Netflix charts with every season released, with the fourth season breaking records on the streaming service. The show has just recently started filming its fifth and final season, getting fans around the world geared up for this ground-breaking original television series.

Related: Stranger Things Moments That Were Totally Unscripted

While the main plot of the show centers around Eleven and her battle with the Upside Down, relationships between the characters are heavily focused on. As unlikely friendships are formed and the characters find lovers, there are several couples that are forged throughout the show.

One of the most prominent relationships is the love triangle between Steve, Nancy, and Jonathan. Natalia Dyer plays Nancy, and she is joined by Joe Keery and Charlie Heaton. Throughout the seasons, we see Nancy’s bond with both boys, inevitably leaving one for the other. In real life, however, Natalia and Charlie are a couple and this is an inside look on their relationship.

8 How Natalia Dyer & Charlie Heaton Met

Charlie Heaton and Natalia Dyer were already practiced actors before joining the Stranger Things cast, racking up seven and nine credits on their resumes (respectively). They first met, however, on the set of the show. Season one brought Natalia, Charlie, and Joe Keery together, often sharing scenes and forming a bond between the actors.

7 When Did Natalia Dyer & Charlie Heaton Officially Start Dating?

Charlie had a girlfriend before meeting Natalia named Akiko Matsuura, whom he had a child with. Thankfully, since their split he has been able to heal and move on. According to US Weekly, sources have confirmed that Natalia and Charlie’s romantic relationship began in 2017, which is also when the two were filming for season two of Stranger Things.

6 Natalia Dyer & Charlie Heaton Have To Navigate Dating While Working Together

While it is likely difficult for any two actors to date due to a demanding work schedule, Natalia and Charlie have to navigate dating in a whole different realm: keeping their acting lives professional and saving their romantic bond for off-set. Dyer shared in an interview that she and Heaton frequently goof off when given the chance, helping to keep things fun and light between takes.

5 Natalia Dyer & Charlie Heaton Love Attending Events Arm-In-Arm

Although the whole of the Stranger Things cast have shown off their impeccable style on red carpets, Charlie Heaton and Natalia Dyer are two of the most fashion-forward of the group. Because of this, fans can often see them attending events like fashion shows and award ceremonies arm-in-arm and dressed to the nines.

4 Is There Tension Between Natalia Dyer & Charlie Heaton?

Both Natalia Dyer and Charlie Heaton strive to be successful in their craft, so tension could easily arise through trying to be the best. The couple, however, is extremely supportive of each other and only want the best for their partner. Following a premier from one of Natalia’s projects, a fellow attendee shared that Charlie looked so proud of his girlfriend and honored to “be on her arm for the night.”

3 Natalia Dyer Opened Up About Her Feelings Regarding Charlie Heaton

In July 2019, Natalia Dyer opened up about how it felt to be working with her boyfriend for the last two years. She shared, “It’s always really fun. We’re really comfortable with each other, so we can play and feel more free, and we can talk about it before [we film the scenes].”

2 The Couple Revealed Their Biggest Opposition Earlier This Year

While couples who date in the eye of the public face many trials, Natalia and Charlie recently shared their biggest struggle. More so than any romantic tensions, the biggest hardship they face is opposition from the public. Not only individual insults thrown out on social media, but people casting shade on the two for not looking “right” together or judging their choices.

1 What Does The Future Look Like For Natalia Dyer & Charlie Heaton?

As of now, Heaton and Dyer are getting ready to film the last season of Stranger Things. This may be the last production they star in together, so they are savoring every moment. Natalia is also working on two other projects; She is acting in two upcoming films entitled Chestnut and All Fun and Games, both of which are in post-production. The couple is taking every day as it comes, with no rush or pressures of a relationship timeline.
