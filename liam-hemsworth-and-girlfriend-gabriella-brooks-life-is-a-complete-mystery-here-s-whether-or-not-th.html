Liam Hemsworth and Miley Cyrus had weathered many storms during their on-and-off again relationship, but when they finally got married in December 2018, fans were convinced this was it. They would be together for the long haul. But that was not the case, and Hemsworth would file for divorce soon after. Since then, both he and Cyrus have moved on.

Hemsworth has been linked to model Gabriella Brooks. Still, unlike his ex-wife's couplings following their split (which include Cody Simpson and Kaitlynn Carter), he has been incredibly secretive about his. This has made his relationship with Brooks even more interesting, but it's also almost impossible to decipher what is happening between them…

When Did Liam Hemsworth And His Girlfriend Gabriella Brooks First Start Dating?

Liam Hemsworth's most well-known relationship was with his now ex-wife, Miley Cyrus. The pair were together off-and-on for a decade. However, after his divorce, Hemsworth returned to his native Australia and appeared to keep a relatively low profile.

During this time, it was also reported that he was in a relationship with Gabriella Brooks (the second woman he was linked to since his breakup, the first being a brief coupling with Australian actress Maddison Brown).

Their timeline can be traced back to December 2019, People reports. This is when the pair were first spotted together at Byron Bay (his divorce would be finalized on Dec. 24, 2019). Gabriella Brooks is also believed to have met Liam Hemsworth's parents around this time. Unlike Miley Cyrus, Brooks is also Australian and is a model.

She and Hemsworth have been photographed together on several outings but only made their public debut as a couple at a fundraising event in June 2021. The pair have chosen to keep their relationship very much out of the public eye, and this includes not sharing their lives on social media. But they are reported to have been serious. "Liam and Gabriella have spent a lot of time in Australia together," a source told People in January 2020.

"Liam has been in a great mood."

This news would have pleased most of his fans because divorce is never easy, and it has even more complications when it has to play out so publicly for the world to see.

In the months that followed, Liam Hemsworth and Gabriella Brooks were snapped at several locations together. It is also reported that they attended a party and quarantined together. Most of their relationship took place in Australia, but the couple also made a big life decision together; to move to Los Angeles.

People Magazine report that in December 2021, the pair moved to Los Angeles together. In 2022, they were still reported to be together, and on January 2023, Gabriella Brooks posted on social media to celebrate her boyfriend's 33rd birthday. And then what happened?

Why Has Liam Hemsworth Chosen To Stay So Quiet About His Relationship?

Although Liam Hemsworth and Gabriella Brooks' relationship appears to have been solid, there were reports toward the end of 2022 that claimed they had broken up. According to J-14, In Touch reported the news, stating that an insider revealed the couple broke up a "couple of months ago."

They have been spotted together since then, though, so the report could have been inaccurate. And if they did split, they got back together. But what have Liam Hemsworth and Gabriella Brooks said about their coupling? The answer is not much. However, Brooks did comment on the importance of her relationship during an interview with Stellar Magazine.

"My personal relationship is very, very important and very sacred to me," Brooks said (via Daily Mail). "And I feel like in an industry where so much is put out on show, there are some things you just want to keep to yourself. I completely understand [the interest]. But at the end of the day, I guess I just like having that for me … But he's great. [The Hemsworths] are great. They are the most beautiful people, and I'm very lucky to know them."

If reports are to be believed, then Liam Hemsworth also really values his relationship, and it has proven to him that he can still find happiness after his divorce. "His family really likes her, and they have a normal relationship without drama," a source previously told E! News.

The fact that they are both Australian is also reported to have solidified their bond. "The Australia connection is definitely nice for both of them," an insider told E! News. "She wants to be in Australia just as much as he does and they both really enjoy being there and spending time with family and friends. He doesn't feel like he is being pulled away."

Are Liam Hemsworth And Gabriella Brooks Still Together?

Liam Hemsworth has chosen to remain quiet about his relationship. As to why this is, we cannot be certain, but given how much attention there was on his coupling with Miley Cyrus, it would not be surprising that he would want to keep his private life as private as possible.

This is not a bad sign, nor does it suggest that Hemsworth and Gabriella Brooks have trouble in their relationship. But their last reported outing happened on Jan. 29, where they were seen on Rodeo Drive in Beverly Hills, Elle reports.

That is several months ago now. Not regularly posting photos of each other on social media or giving interviews about their romance will also result in less pressure being placed on the relationship. But it is also for this reason that their coupling it is still a mystery to many, and we cannot say with certainty whether they are even still together.
