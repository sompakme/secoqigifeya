Trey Smith (Will Smiths Son) Biography, Age, Wiki, Height, Weight, Girlfriend, Family &amp; More

The son of actor Will Smith and his first wife, actress Sheree Zampino, Willard Carroll “Trey” Smith III, is an American disc jockey and actor who, despite being the oldest child of one of the biggest stars of Hollywood grew up in relative obscurity.

He got the opportunity to enjoy a rather normal upbringing while his half-siblings endured the immense pressure of being constantly under the spotlight. According to Smith, following the divorce, his son felt that he had betrayed and abandoned him and it inevitably affected their relationship.

Trey is often photographed with his father, and stepmother Jada Pinkett Smith but it took years to build back a loving relationship with each other. Primarily active in the Los Angeles area he has adopted the professional name AcE as a disc jockey.

Under the name AcE at Club Liv in Miami, Trey made his debut as a disc jockey in December 2012. He served as the DJ at the Vanity Fair pre-Oscar party in February 2013. Since then, he has played his sets in various venues all over the world and has established himself as one of the leading DJs in Los Angeles.

Including ‘Collection’ (2010), ‘Orwell’ (2012), and ‘Cocoa’ (2012), he has put out several extended plays over the years. Directed by Pinkett Smith and featuring Jaden and Willow he released a music video ‘Find You Somewhere’ in the year 2016.

In the 1998 music video for his father’s track, ‘Just the Two of Us’ Trey made his first appearance as an actor. Two episodes of ‘All of Us’, a UPN (later The CW) sitcom featured him in 2003. In the short film ‘The Speech’ he shared screen space with Trevor Gretzky in 2008.
