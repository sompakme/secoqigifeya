Scooby-Doo and WWE: Curse of the Speed Demon

The following video is the new trailer for the upcoming WWE / Hanna Barbera collaboration, Scooby-Doo and WWE: Curse of the Speed Demon. 

The animated film, featuring several WWE Superstars such as Triple H, The Undertaker, Sheamus, Paige and more, will be available on Blu-Ray, DVD and Digital HD on August 9, 2016, just in time for WWE SummerSlam.
